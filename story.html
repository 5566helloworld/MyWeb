Story of this World

A long time ago, on this ancient continent, an energy substance was discovered that some claimed was a remnant of the God particle from the ancient times when God created the world and could provide unlimited energy.

It had been buried for a long time, but one day it was discovered by chance, but because it could not be dug up, it was only marked, and the news caused an uproar among the academic elite of the world. The four nations on this continent set out to obtain the God Particle, one by one, with their own armies, in a bid to seize it, and a world war began.

Republic of Goliath

Magic, technology - these elements are common in this landlocked country and do not conflict with each other at all. The Republic of Goliath came into being in the 15th century after the hanging of the infamous King Lucius, and began a prosperous industrial revolution and foreign trade, with workers pouring barrels and barrels of

For the Goliaths, it was commonplace to enjoy the magic of riding in gas carriages and tapping their fingers to light cigarettes, and the logic of coal-fired automata was not incompatible with the mystery of magic. At the same time, magical schools and technical colleges have always provided the nation with talent, from performing evil sacrifices on the battlefield to enhance metal tanks powered by magic stones without the knowledge of the Church, to artificial rainfall in the Republic, the coexistence of technology and magic has always been a strength of the nation.

Religion and God have always been present in this scientific nation, and from the horrors of the House of Lucius to the Republican period of the Industrial Revolution, the Church has remained neutral, providing help to the poor and cultivating God's servants. Recently, the Church's preachers found an artefact in the Plain of Kanker that overturned the Church's beliefs and in order to cover it up, the Church asked the Council to go on an expedition to remove the blasphemous artefact from the name of God. The Council also wanted to prove to its neighbours that it was strong after the Industrial Revolution by going to the Plain of Kanker, so both sides agreed to go to war and so the bloody battle on the Plain of Kanker began.

Calamity

In order to win the war, a group of cultists and the Ministry of Defence have made a private agreement to exchange the souls of condemned prisoners with demons to obtain an extremely powerful team of evil powers. As a former Republican soldier with blood on his hands, he is so desperate to kill that the army has to deploy him to the front of the line to prevent him from hurting his own men. Of course, the price of trading with the devil is that the squad's sanity will be worn away in one kill after another until they die, and their souls will then become the devil's fuel, so they will always be trapped in an endless war, and death is the only place they will end up. As for Calamity, he was looking forward to the great battle at the Plain of Kanker.

Angel

The Queen of the Greinch Empire was disappeared, and it is suspected that she was assassinated by noblemen who seized control of the country from the royal family. For Angel, she has never known her birth. Her adoptive father, Pierre, the former first scientist of the Empire, saved Angel as a baby from the palace massacre, thus saving the daughter of the Queen, the last of the Empire's magical bloodline. They fled to the Republic of Goliath, where they stayed together for the rest of their lives. To help her, he designed a set of magic- powered wings that made Angel a true Valkyrie. When the Kanker Campaign begins, Angel is ordered by the Republic to join it. She is excited to go to war, not knowing that she will be slaughtering her former people on the battlefield.

Greinch Empire

"Glory to the Queen", a phrase that every citizen of the Greinch Empire must have heard. As they worked in the dangerous refining of whale oil, as they worked all night with their children in the textile mills, this phrase was at the end of every broadcast. It was illegal to stop the radio and refuse to listen to it in the Empire, and serious cases were sent to the northern mines for life, where the people-centred Queen was the Queen's head on their hard-earned coins. Police posts were scattered throughout the cities, ports and factories of any empire, on the outskirts of cities and even on outlying islands far from the gathering places, the broadcasting systems and automatic turrets that stood in the streets and fields to guard and monitor the behaviour of every citizen, who was registered at birth in the central information system of the capital, the barrel-driven punch cards that recorded the information of every citizen, the differential machines that calculated the potential of every The dumb and the dumber are fed from an early age with repetitive processes under iron skins, while those with potential are educated to become engineers or police officers.

As for the managers of this high-pressure country, they spend their days in the rich neighbourhoods, enjoying the privileges of the elite, who are not created equal, so it is only natural that they accept this fact and manage the main political and business affairs of the empire. Of course, there must have been people who could not stand the high levels of work, who rebelled and the police went to suppress them, not once or twice anyway. The metal armoured vehicles assembled from the bloodied hands of the workers would burn whale oil to crush the workers who had assembled them, and the workers themselves would never have a chance to touch their bosses.

Corrad

The House family, which provides the Empire's vital energy and is also the largest shareholder in the Imperial Armed Forces, is a family of cannibals who have squeezed countless workers out of the Empire, and the family's youngest son, Corrad, is one of the few who cares about his workers. As opposed to his own spendthrift family, Kreider would stoop to inspect the workers in the factory, so when the whale-oil factory overloaded and exploded, his workers risked their lives to rescue Corrad from the flames. With 90% of his body burnt, Corrad is stuffed by his family into the latest exoskeleton armour designed by the military. The iron coffin restores Corrad 's mobility, but the pain of the burns warps his sanity. He hates his brutal and cold-blooded family, and he hates the cold-blooded machinery of the state, after all, the very system that gave the Haus family the right to exploit them, and they are all responsible for the factory accident. The God Particle is his weapon of choice to overthrow the country, and he wants to seize this ancient artefact to overthrow this twisted nation.

Leiter

There is a legend of a black shadow monster, a three-metre-tall man in iron armour who suddenly rushes out of the battlefield wielding a huge, powerful hammer that can smash the brains out of anyone who sees it. The Black Gauntlet is a legendary terror on the battlefield because no one has ever lived to see it in its full glory. The Iron Man himself was the secret weapon of the Greinch Empire, powered by whale-oil alloy armour and wearing a finely crafted power hammer that turned the wearer into a super soldier. The armour's high cost made it a unique weapon for fighting morale. His drivers have passed from generation to generation, becoming the vanguard of the Empire's warriors, while Leiter, the living man beneath the armour, once a boxer, now charges into battle for the Empire, and will once again crush the enemy mercilessly on the Plain of Kanker.

Sky Kingdom

Since time immemorial, man has longed to roam the skies without restraint, so as the waters rose each year, Genesian scientists and engineers filled the foundations of their cities with helium, using the few particles of God as fuel to power them, and the world's first sky city was born. The world's first city in the sky was born, and one by one, many cities in Genesia were freed from the bonds of gravity, and many cities joined them to become a kingdom in the sky. The original people of Genesia loved the arts and opera. There was hardly a person in the capital who could not sing, and their love of theatre and entertainment led them to invite musicians and artists from all over the world to become inhabitants of the Sky Kingdom. Thanks to the early God particle engine, which gave them unlimited energy, and the development of automated technology, the people of the city have been involved in the arts and sciences for almost all of their lives, and magic has only been used to complement their artistic performances. As for the terrestrial nations, many of them challenged the status of the Sky Kingdom, but their mastery of air conversion technology enabled them to produce tens of thousands of tonnes of chlorine gas to easily destroy the opposing terrestrial nations.

Recently, some of the other nations on the ground have unearthed additional God particles in the Plain of Kanker. The Sky Kingdom intercepted this information in a deal with the Earth, and their House of Representatives immediately authorized military action against the Plain of Kanker. For the only Sky Kingdom , they are determined not to allow another Sky Kingdom to be created, so they are determined to win the Plain of Kanker.

Rosa Syndica Alliance

In the far north of the Eurasian continent lies a primitive land, covered in snow and ice all year round, where goods are scarce and the environment is harsh. The Slavs have a primitive life there, and in order to survive the hardships, they have the most devout faith in the cruel gods of nature. The Arctic is a vast country and the Slavs are therefore divided into different tribes, the three largest of which have their own distinctive characteristics.

The Woodlanders, who wielded the primordial power of earth and wood, and who, by the grace of their Mother of Trees, knew how to control plants and use the walls of roots to protect themselves and produce food.

The Moonchasers, the most brutal and violent of the Slavs, hunt and pillage for a living, and some of them forcibly change their bodies by drawing on the magic of the moon to become transmitted centaurs and werewolves.

The Arabs, located in the inhabitable zone of the Arctic Circle, were originally the most skilled of the Slavic peoples in commerce and trade, learning about the civilised world through foreign trade and gradually building their own city-states and civilisations, using ancient magic and the flexibility of technology to improve their lives.

The tribes were separated from each other until the Sky Kingdom arrived from afar and slaughtered the Slavic continent for the rare God particle in the Arctic Circle. The tribes suffered heavy losses during the cataclysm, and in order to fight off foreign enemies, the chiefs, shamans and leaders of each tribe came together to unite the remaining forces of each tribe and form the Rosa Syndica Alliance.

Hulse

No one has ever seen Hulse's true face, a half-horse shifter who wears silver armour even in camp, under which he rarely speaks. Some say he is a hunter from the Woodland clan who knows how to use nature to his advantage to ambush his enemies, while others say he is an orphan from the holocaust, adopted by the Moonchaser clan as a child, who understands the language of the beasts and controls their charge. This half-horse shifter, who could control the power of earth and wood and the power of fierce beasts, became one of the most elite warriors of the Rosa Syndica Alliance, a cunning barbarian for the enemy and a hope in desperate times for the friendlies. So for the battle of the Kanker, this silver shadow will once again turn the tide.

Falcon

After the devastation of the Rosa Syndica Alliance in the air, the representatives of the Arabs pointed out the disadvantages of the Alliance in terms of air weapons. The Alliance therefore eagerly reversed the armour left behind by the Sky Kingdom in the hope of producing units that could also fight in the air. As a result, the first air squad was formed, thanks to the best efforts of the scientists. The leader of the squad, the Falcon, was the perfect match for the Iron Wings, displaying an amazing talent for flying among a squad with no previous flying experience, and being a muscle-bound Moonchaser who retained a great mind and excellent leadership and command skills, he deservedly became the leader of the squad. So when they go into action on the Plain of Kanker, they will also take direct orders from the Alliance Council in the form of a special squad and carry out decapitation operations against their enemies. For their enemies, the black shadows in the air will be the last thing they see.
