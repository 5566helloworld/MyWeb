Winning Condition

The player's objective is to occupy the capture point and gained the capture score. Of course, you can also employ various means to eliminate enemy units or slay enemy heroes. However, simultaneously, you must also be mindful of protecting your own controlled hero because once they are slain, you lose the game.

Victory Conditions (Achieve any one of the following):

  1. Gain 3 capture points.
  2. Be the last surviving player.

Preparation

  1. Before the game begins players roll dice to determine the turn order and place map tiles accordingly. Then, players draw different map fragments. They can assemble a 3x3 map by arranging these fragments around the map tiles they possess. Players can rotate the map to determine terrain as well as the positions of treasure points and supply depots, thereby formulating different strategies. (Note: Map tiles with capture points must be in the center.)
  2. After players have finalized the terrain, they can choose their hero and recruit soldiers using the initial funding provided by the hero.
  3. Place teh faction identification base under the soldier pawns and on the soldier cards, and place the hero at their initial point to begin the game.

Game Procedure and Specific Rules

Turn Sturcture

Each turn proceeds in the same order. After the end of the player, the turn will be passed to the next player according to the order.

>Start stage

  • At this stage, if any of your unit is in the capture point, you can get 1 capture score.

>Action stage

  • Action Points

    Each player has 3 action points for each turn. Action taken by a player will cost them 1 action point, including: "setting up soldier", "moving soldier and attacking", "moving hero and attacking", and "using hero skills". Each time an action is taken as above, one action point is deducted and when the action points for the turn are exhausted, it goes into the End Stage.
  • Moving Pawns

    A player can control the movement of his own pawn, which can only be made in a vertical direction, not in an angled direction. A pawn can only move within its maximum movement range, for example, if the movement range is 4, then it is allowed to move 0-4 paces but no more than 4 steps. After moving, the unit can can attack any enemy unit within its attack range, or just end its moving action, then cost one action point. The same rules apply to the movement of heroes as above.
  • Attack Action and Damge Calculation

    Attack range is calculated based on vertical movement, not angled movement. The damage dealt by an attack is based on the combined attack power of the unit, equipment cards and hero's skills.

    When a unit takes damage, place the corresponding damage counter on the corresponding unit card. When a unit's HP reaches 0 or below, it is considered eliminated. Remove the corresponding pawn from the board, discard the unit card, and discard the equipping equipment card. If a hero's HP reaches 0 or below, the player is considered out of the game, and all units controlled by that player are removed from the board.
  • Counter Attack

    If the attacked unit's attack range includes the unit initiating the attack, a counter attack is triggered. When receiving damage, the attacking unit also receives a counter attack calculation. The counter attack rules for heroes are the same as above.


  • Soldier

    Each player can have a maximum of 4 soldier. Each soldier has its own faction identification base, which has a symbol and different colors on it to help identify and track the damage taken of the soldier. The base have symbols 1 , 2 , 3 , 4 , which is used to identifies which soldier pawn on the field belong to. These faction identification base appear on both the soldier pieces and the soldier cards, with the soldier pawn placed on the board and the corresponding soldier cards placed in the player's place. The cards show the type of soldier, attack power, HP, attack range and movement range.
    Further detail of soldier can be seen in this page.
  • Deploying Soldier

    The player can spend one action point and the corresponding funds to recruit a soldier at the initial point.
  • Using Hero's Active Skill

    During a player‚Äòs turn, the player can spend one action point to use the hero‚Äôs active skill. After using an active skill, the hero cannot move again and the player must place a cooldown indicator on the hero card that corresponds to the hero‚Äòs active skill cooldown. At the end stage, the cooldown indicator is counted down by 1. If the count is reduced to 0, the cooldown indicator is removed. The player can only use active skills when the hero does not have a cooldown indicator.
  • Using Treasure Card

    During the player's turn, the player can use any treasure card without spending action point.
    Further detail of treasure cards can be seen in this page.
  • Triggering Treasure Points / Supply Points

    During a player's turn, when a unit stop on a treasure/supply point, a terrain effect is triggered after the attack has been calculated. A unit that is already on the same treasure point/supply point must leave the terrain before the terrain effect can be triggered again.

>End Stage

  • At this stage, you can counted down the cooldown indicator by 1 and pass the turn to the next player.

Terrain



  • Capture Point

    A 3x3 area in the centre of the map, where a player gains an capture score if any of his/her unit is in the capture point at his/her start stage.


  • Forest

    Heroes, Infantry, Rider, Sniper and Medics take 2 less damage here and tanks cannot enter. Sniper and tanks cannot cause damage to those heroes and soldier which located in this area except airforce unit.



  • River

    Required 2 steps to leave this area (if there are not enough steps left, you cannot leave)


  • Mountain

    The soldiers cannot pass through and can intercept long-range attacks (i.e. the attacker cannot attack on the target if the attacker is separated from it by a mountain)



  • Treasure Point

    When you step onto this terrain, draw a treasure card. If a trap card is drawn, reveal it and apply its effect to the unit that get that card.



  • Supply Point

    Once on this area you will receive 200 funds
