Normal Magic

Forbidden Magic

Normal Equipment

Each unit can only equip 1 equipment card; it cannot be removed after equiped; it can be replaced with a new equipment card and the originally equipped equipment card will be discarded.

Artifact

Each unit can only equip 1 equipment card; it cannot be removed after equiped; it can be replaced with a new equipment card and the originally equipped equipment card will be discarded. Each player can only equip one artifact for his/her units.

Trap Cards

When obtaining a trap card, it must be disclosed and the effect must be executed immediately.
